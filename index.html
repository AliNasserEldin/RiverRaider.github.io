RIVER RAIDER

This is a remake for rive raider made by me, the original game was made by Activision corp for atari (1982). look below kindly for more details.

the game starts with a list of game modes(normal, easy, hacker), instructions, information and exit button

easy mode : ships and planes are moving vertically

normal mode : ships and planes are moving horizontally and vertically

hacker mode : you can't die made to test the game and understand it's rules better

you must give your name to let the game memorize the heightest score and it's owner.

game also pops up a window to greet you with your name before it starts.

top left the screen the game lists all information that concerns you(your score, timer, your fuel status, tour lives)

ships and planes positions are generated randomly in easy mode

whenever a player blows a fuel tank the fuel tank destoys the nearby ships and planes

after your score exceeds 2000 your lives will be increased by 1

whenever you hit plane or ship with plane's body your lives are decreased by 1

River sides are generated randomly with the (ships, planes, fuel tanks) in mind.
